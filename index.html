Omelette Recipe

Recetas Perfectas

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

10 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

1/4 cup whole milk.

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt.

2 tablespoons unsalted butter.

Intructions

Beat the eggs and milk until ligth and frothy.

Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.

Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.

Use a spatula to push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet toward.

When the eggs are mostly set but stukk soft, fold in half.

Slide the omellete onto a plate.
